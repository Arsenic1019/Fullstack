Ingredients

  • Rice flour - 2 cups
  • Jaggery - 1 cup
  • Sesame seeds - 2 tbsp
  • Water - 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Mix rice flour with water to make dough.
  2. Prepare jaggery filling with sesame seeds.
  3. Shape dough and add filling.
  4. Steam for 20 minutes.
